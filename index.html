Victoya Venise

Victoya Venise (@superempress) is a WriteHer, DesignHer and LoveHer (activist/youth-mentor) based in Brooklyn, NY. She is the founder of SisterCircleBK, a mentorship movement for girls living in high-risk communities in New York City that teaches and empowers youth through art. By teaching classes and workshops on yoga, meditation, sewing and DIY crafts, she seeks to teach girls their own worth and power. She works with Urban Strategies SONYC Program as well as Reel Works. She was featured in Proof Media's 2015 Social Picture Justice Project focusing on mass incarceration as well as a member of the Social Intervention Research Group at Columbia University. Her philosophy is based on the notion that 'women can empower women, who can then empower children and men'.
